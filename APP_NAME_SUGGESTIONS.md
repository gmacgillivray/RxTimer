# Workout Timer Application - Name Suggestions

## Date: 2025-01-18

## Research Summary

### Existing CrossFit Timer App Names (Competitive Analysis)

**Popular Apps in Market**:
1. **Box Timer** - Simple, CrossFit gym reference
2. **SmartWOD Timer** - "Smart" + WOD (Workout of the Day)
3. **Captime** - Capability + Time (portmanteau)
4. **O'Coach** - Personal coach reference
5. **SugarWOD** - Sweet/easy WOD reference
6. **Beyond the Whiteboard** - Metaphorical, planning-focused
7. **Workout Timer: Crossfit WODs** - Descriptive, SEO-focused

### Naming Patterns Observed

**Pattern 1: Timer/Time + Modifier**
- Box Timer, WOD Timer, HIIT Timer
- Direct, functional, immediately clear purpose

**Pattern 2: Portmanteaus/Compound Words**
- SmartWOD, Captime, SugarWOD
- Memorable, brandable, unique

**Pattern 3: Metaphorical/Aspirational**
- Beyond the Whiteboard
- Evokes feeling/goal rather than function

**Pattern 4: Descriptive/SEO**
- "Workout timer: Crossfit WODs"
- Clear but less brandable

**Common Elements**:
- WOD (Workout of the Day) - CrossFit terminology
- Box - CrossFit gym reference
- Timer/Time/Clock - Core functionality
- Smart/Pro/Cap - Quality indicators

## Name Suggestion Categories

### Category A: Clean & Simple (Recommended for iOS)

**Minimalist, Apple App Store Style**

1. **RepsTimer**
   - *Rationale*: Simple, memorable, references CrossFit rep counting
   - *Pros*: Short, clean, easy to say/spell
   - *Cons*: May seem generic
   - *Similar to*: Box Timer pattern

2. **Metcon** (Metabolic Conditioning)
   - *Rationale*: CrossFit term for conditioning workouts
   - *Pros*: Industry-specific, one word, strong
   - *Cons*: May be unfamiliar to beginners
   - *Similar to*: Captime brevity

3. **Tempo**
   - *Rationale*: Pace/rhythm of workouts, musical reference
   - *Pros*: Beautiful word, universal understanding, brandable
   - *Cons*: Not explicitly fitness-related
   - *Uniqueness*: High - less common in timer space

4. **Cadence**
   - *Rationale*: Rhythm and timing, training cadence
   - *Pros*: Professional sound, workout association
   - *Cons*: Longer word, harder to type
   - *Similar to*: Tempo concept

5. **Interval**
   - *Rationale*: Core concept of EMOM/interval training
   - *Pros*: Direct, clear, simple
   - *Cons*: Possibly generic
   - *Uniqueness*: Medium

### Category B: CrossFit-Specific Branding

**Appeals directly to CrossFit community**

6. **WODClock**
   - *Rationale*: WOD + Clock, straightforward
   - *Pros*: Clear target audience, SEO-friendly
   - *Cons*: Similar to existing SmartWOD Timer
   - *Risk*: Moderate - established pattern

7. **The WOD Timer** (or just "WOD")
   - *Rationale*: Simple, direct, definitive article adds authority
   - *Pros*: Clear purpose, easy to find
   - *Cons*: Very generic, hard to trademark
   - *Risk*: High - many similar names

8. **BoxClock**
   - *Rationale*: Box (CrossFit gym) + Clock
   - *Pros*: CrossFit-specific, simple
   - *Cons*: Very similar to Box Timer (existing app)
   - *Risk*: High - trademark concerns

9. **RxTimer** (Rx = "as prescribed" in CrossFit)
   - *Rationale*: Rx is CrossFit term for standard/prescribed workout
   - *Pros*: Community recognition, short
   - *Cons*: May confuse non-CrossFitters
   - *Uniqueness*: High within timer space

10. **AMRAPclock**
    - *Rationale*: Highlights key timer mode
    - *Pros*: Descriptive, memorable
    - *Cons*: Only mentions one mode
    - *Uniqueness*: Medium

## Naming Strategy Recommendations

**Primary Name + Subtitle Pattern**:
- **Primary**: Short, memorable, brandable (1-2 words)
- **Subtitle**: Descriptive, SEO-friendly (keywords)

**Examples**:
- **Atlas** - CrossFit & HIIT Timer
- **Forge** - WOD Timer for Athletes
- **Tempo** - AMRAP, EMOM & For Time

## Market Positioning

### Premium Positioning (Atlas, Forge, Kinetic)
- **Price Point**: $4.99-9.99 or subscription
- **Messaging**: "Professional-grade timing"
- **Audience**: Serious athletes, CrossFit competitors
- **Differentiation**: Design, precision, features

### Community Positioning (Metcon, RxTimer)
- **Price Point**: $2.99-4.99 or freemium
- **Messaging**: "Built for athletes, by athletes"
- **Audience**: CrossFit community, box regulars
- **Differentiation**: Community features, authenticity

### Broad Appeal (Tempo, Cadence, RepsTimer)
- **Price Point**: Free with IAP or $1.99-3.99
- **Messaging**: "For every workout, every athlete"
- **Audience**: Fitness enthusiasts, multi-sport
- **Differentiation**: Ease of use, versatility

## Final Recommendation

**Top Choice**: **Atlas** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**Reasoning**:
1. **Memorable**: Single word, powerful mythology
2. **Brandable**: Rich story potential, visual metaphor

5. **Scalable**: Can expand beyond timers (Atlas Fitness ecosystem)
6. **International**: Works globally, easy to pronounce
7. **App Store**: Short, searches well, stands out
