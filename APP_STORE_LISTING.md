### Notes for Reviewer
```
RxTimer is a workout timer for CrossFit-style training.

To test the app:
1. Open the app and select a timer type (AMRAP recommended)
2. Configure duration (default 10 minutes is fine)
3. Tap "Start Workout" to enter the timer screen
4. Observe that the timer shows the configured time (10:00) in idle state
5. Tap the green "Start" button to begin the countdown
6. Tap "Complete Round" to track round splits
7. Let timer run or tap "Finish" to end workout
8. View workout summary with total time and round splits
9. Access History from sidebar to view saved workouts

Background audio mode keeps timer running when screen is locked (required for gym use).

All features are accessible in the free version.
```

## Frequently Asked Questions (for App Store & Support)

**Q: Does RxTimer work when my screen is locked?**
A: Yes! RxTimer uses background audio mode to keep timing when your screen is locked. Perfect for gym use.

**Q: Can I track my workout history?**
A: Yes, RxTimer automatically saves all completed workouts with round splits and total time.

**Q: Is there a subscription?**
A: No subscriptions, ever. RxTimer is a one-time purchase with all features included.

**Q: What timer modes are supported?**
A: AMRAP (countdown), EMOM (intervals), and For Time (count-up with optional cap).

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: Yes! RxTimer works completely offline. No internet required.

**Q: Is it accessible for visually impaired users?**
A: Yes, RxTimer has full VoiceOver support and meets WCAG AA accessibility standards.
